My Special Lady

I'm writing this poem for a very special lady,
Always and forever, She'll always be my baby.
In good times and bad, I won't love her any less

The way I feel about her, Words can't express,
She's so beautiful, She knocks me off my feet.
If loving her is wrong, then I don't want to be right

I am dreaming of holding her, in my arms so tight,
This absolutely special lady means so much to me,
She goes by the most beautiful name, Harira.
